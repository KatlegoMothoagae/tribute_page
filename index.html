  • Voiced by Clancy Brown: Mr. Krabs is voiced by Clancy Brown, a talented actor known for his deep, gravelly voice which adds depth and character to the crustacean's persona.
  • Owner of the Krusty Krab: Mr. Krabs is the proud owner of the Krusty Krab, the most popular fast food joint in Bikini Bottom where SpongeBob works as a fry cook.
  • Obsession with Money: Mr. Krabs is famously obsessed with money. He is known for his iconic catchphrase, "I love money," and will go to great lengths to save or make a few bucks.
  • Secret Recipe: The Krabby Patty secret formula is the heart of Mr. Krabs' success. He guards it fiercely and goes to extreme lengths to protect it from falling into the wrong hands.
  • Thrifty Nature: Despite his wealth, Mr. Krabs is notoriously thrifty. He's always looking for ways to cut costs and maximize profits, often at the expense of his employees' comfort.
  • Naval Career: Before becoming a restaurateur, Mr. Krabs served in the navy. His navy days occasionally resurface in his personality, especially his penchant for order and discipline.
  • Father of Pearl: Pearl is Mr. Krabs' teenage daughter. Despite their differences (she's a whale, he's a crab), Mr. Krabs loves her dearly and often worries about her well-being.
